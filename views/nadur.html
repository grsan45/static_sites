About Nadur

Nadur is a custom world generator for Minecraft version 1.16.2. It is based on the recently added world generation features which allow (nearly) fully customized worlds to be created.
Nadur adds, as of now, 7 new land biomes, 5 oceans, and 2 beaches, with more biomes to come
